01 · Identity

A senior consulting voice, set in serif and Arabic.

The Khatib brand presents a solo Beirut-based practice in structural, geotechnical, and forensic civil engineering — patent-holding, peer-reviewed, OEA-registered. The visual language is engineered documentation: bilingual by default, evidence-led, technically precise without becoming stiff. Inspired by the Ibrahim Energy Partners system; distinct in mark, motif, and motto.

07 · Honesty Rules

Brand-critical. Non-negotiable.

The research dossier flagged real positioning risks — credential inflation, over-claimed IP coverage, namesake confusion with Khatib & Alami. These rules exist to keep every Khatib document defensible.

Patents are Lebanese-registered. Until verified PCT/USPTO/EPO certificates are produced, every reference says “registered in Lebanon.” Never “internationally patented” or “global IP.”
Patent numbers go on the page only when the client supplies them. No invented numbers, no approximations.
OEA-Beirut registration number appears on every external document. Placeholder until supplied.
“26+ years experience” is replaced with named project references. Either name the projects (with client and date), or do not claim the years.
SPSC Ambassador appears as a small membership badge, not in the hero. It is a free volunteer-community badge, not an accredited certification.
Editorial / reviewer roles are listed by named journal (IWA, Heliyon, Case Studies in Construction Materials, KSCE JCE, EJECE), not as an aggregate “18+ journals” claim.
No aggregate citation count displayed. Publications listed individually with DOIs.
No stock imagery. Only authentic project, conference, and patent-demonstration photos.
Civilian engineering brand. Per Dr. Khatib’s explicit WhatsApp directive (May 2026), no military service or military-adjacent framing on any public material. The civilian engineering record — academic, patents, publications, services — carries the brand on its own.
Disambiguation from Khatib & Alami. Every external document carries the full practice name, “Dr. Milad Khatib · Civil Engineering Consultancy.”
