## Cadence recommendation

Four posts across **four to five weeks**, not four days. Tuesday or Wednesday morning Beirut time, one post per week:

- **Week 1:** Research & Publications (the book launch lead — high-prestige, easy to engage with, leads engagement)
- **Week 2:** Structural (most common inquiry trigger)
- **Week 3:** Geotechnical (showcases published research, attracts ministry / water-authority eyeballs)
- **Week 4:** Forensic (highest-value retainer category — comes last, lands on a warmed-up audience)

Then take a week off. Use that week to respond to any inquiries the four posts triggered.

After the first cycle, switch to a quarterly cadence — one substantive piece per quarter, not weekly churn. The brand's compounding asset is *infrequent posts of unusual quality*, not feed presence. Mix in a publication announcement when each new paper is accepted; the book is the pinned post.

## What's explicitly NOT in this pack

- **Hashtag-heavy variants.** This brand does not do feed-optimised hashtag piles.
- **Engagement-bait copy.** No "agree?" or "what do you think?" closes.
- **Carousel templates.** Single-image posts will outperform carousels for a senior consulting voice in this audience.
- **Story / Reel formats.** Out of register for the brand.
- **Any reference to military service, medals, training courses, units, or postings** — per Dr. Khatib's WhatsApp directive of 2 May 2026.
